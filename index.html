Avoriaz

As the highest resort in the porte du soliel, Avoriaz has runs to suit all levels. Famous for the 'Swiss Wall' one of the worlds steepest runs,

Morzine

Nestled in the valley below Avoriaz is the town of Morzine. Sitting between Avoriaz and Les Gets, the town of Morzine has ski in/out and great restaurants.

Les Gets

Les Gets sits at the beginning of the valley. The town is quieter than Morzine and the skiing runs more blue/reds ideal for family
